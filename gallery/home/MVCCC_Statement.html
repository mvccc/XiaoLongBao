Statement Regarding Pastor Daniel Tan’s Departure from MVCCC


To Whom It May Concern,

Recently, it has come to our attention that a rumor is being spread in the Kingdom of God, accusing some brothers and sisters at Mountain View Chinese Christian Church (MVCCC) forced out the beloved Pastor Daniel Tan (陈道明牧师) from MVCCC back several years ago. We hereby declare such rumor is completely false and we condemn such falsehood in the strongest term possible.

Attached with this letter are two resignation letters from Pastor Tan himself. These letters gave clear reasons for his departure from MVCCC.


God bless!

MVCCC Elders and Deacons Board
August 21, 2018
